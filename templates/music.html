  • Biography

    Never Been to Berlin is an electronic live act duo by Goya Buitenhuis and me. We make English-language dance music that draws inspiration from a wide spectrum of genres.

    We started working on this project in the summer of 2019. Our debut album, 'Aha-Erlebnis,' was released 2020. In 2022, we unveiled our EP 'Junction,'. In September 2023 we released our EP 'One Step Back'.

    So far we've done a bunch of live shows across the Netherlands and Europe. From Grasnapolsky to Bevrijdingsfestival Groningen, we always aim to turn every show into an unforgettable experience.

  • Biography

    Midnight Phone Carl was a solo project I worked on while I was suffering from depression during the pandemic. Not being allowed to perform live influenced my music to be softer and more focussed on a solo listening experience. I have not worked on this project for a while since it did not get a lot of attention, perhaps in the future I will get back into the vibe for this again.
