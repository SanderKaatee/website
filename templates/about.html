Who am I?

My name is Sander Kaatee (born 1996). I hold a Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Groningen. I've been producing music since the age of 14, some of which you can listen to and read about on my music page. In addition to music production, I also engage in programming projects that interest me. You can explore these in my projects section. You're welcome to view my CV here or check out my LinkedIn profile. If you'd like to get in contact, feel free to send me an email.

Why this website

I wanted a place to display the things I do, and I hope to add more over time. I also sort of dislike how centralized the Internet has become; most of what we do online is in the hands of a few companies like Google, Microsoft, Meta, etc. I believe the proprietary nature of software owned by companies that play such a crucial role in our society carries significant inherent risk. Therefore, this website serves as a small step away from centralized social media. I built the website using Flask and JavaScript. You can find the source code on my GitHub. It's based on a template by Nicola Tolin; you can find out more in the credits section.
