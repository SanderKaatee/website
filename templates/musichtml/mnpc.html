• Biography

    Midnight Phone Carl was a solo project I worked on while I was suffering from depression during the pandemic. Not being allowed to perform live influenced my music to be softer and more focussed on a solo listening experience. I have not worked on this project for a while since it did not get a lot of attention, perhaps in the future I will get back into the vibe for this again.
